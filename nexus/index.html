Nexus

An app for players made by a player.

Nexus is an app for you to follow the champions available in the free rotation of League of Legends week. Also, learn more about their biographies and save your favorite skin on your device. See also your nickname, summoner icon, and level.

Have fun!


Previous Versions:

The first version enabled players to check the Champions Free Rotation of the week according to the Player Region and access some information about the champion.

The second version enabled the Players to check all Champions available in the game. Also, it allowed the Player to filter the Champions by lane or role and free rotation. Review the Spells available and their description. And create or import an arsenal.
